How Berserk helped me see through the dark

Berserk and my depression.

In the early summer of ‘97 when I was coming home from my 8th grade class trip to Washington D.C. that both my twin sister and I went on, it was she who first noticed that the way we were going home wasn’t the usual way. After that, my older sister and my mother broke down and told us that we were evicted and now living in the basement of a family friend we lost everything all my clothes, toys, and personal belongings it sucked That was when my first real obsession with Anime started, it was at first just an escape from my terrible situation, anything to distract me from my home life and my start as an angsty teenager.
