How Berserk changed my life

I love Anime and Manga but none has had as deep of an effect on me personally than Berserk by Kentaro Miura. Who sadly passed away on May 6th, 2021.

Although it's notorious for its over the top use of explicit violence and heavy themes, His dark fantasy epic helped me a lot when I was depressed and going through some of the hardest times in my life.
